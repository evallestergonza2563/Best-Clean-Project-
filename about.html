ABOUT OUR COMMERCIAL GROOMING COMPANY

A heritage of excellence.
In 1929, Marion E. Wade, a former minor league baseball player, founded ServiceMaster as a moth protection organization based in Chicago, Illinois. Just 23 years later, it expanded to include franchised carpet cleaning services. Over the years, ServiceMaster Clean has expanded to provide a wide range of exceptional cleaning services for a variety of commercial and residential properties.

Relocating its headquarters to Memphis, Tennessee from time to time in 2007, ServiceMaster Clean is now a world-class company providing professional cleaning services to more than 75,000 homes and businesses every day.

WE ARE ALWAYS LOOKING FOR PERFECTION

For over 65 years, we have served a wide variety of consumers, from homeowners to facility and property managers, and healthcare decision makers. Our relentless passion for excellence and customer service means we continually invest in improving our own processes, our people and our interactions with every organization we serve.

Meet Our Team

We are an experienced team of workers, administrators, organizers and professionals. Our team is dedicated to the professional cleaning of different types of places, always providing high quality in our services.
